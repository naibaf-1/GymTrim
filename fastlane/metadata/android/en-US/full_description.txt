This app is a small fitness tracker. It's supposed to help track training data
  and see your progress, but there is no warranty that it will improve your
  athleticism or health.

  Features:
    - Store Plans & Exercises
    - Training Mode
    - Track Your Progress
    - Different calculators
    - Material You design
    - Auto-save, search, swipe-to-delete
    - English and German translations
    - Light and dark mode
    - Import and export data

  Feel free to contribute and report bugs. Feature requests are welcome too.
